Our history and what we stand for

The first Emerald Grove restaurant opened in 1967 in San Francisco.
The original idea was to provide healthy and fresh food while following the cycle of the seasons.
This concept was a success and a second restaurant was opened in Los Angeles
in the early 1970's. The 21st century saw the expansion of the franchise
to the East coast with openings in the capital and in New York City.

We do not only care about the environment and the health of our custommers,
we also take pride in the working conditions and the well being of our staff.
This is the reason why we pay a living wage to all of our employees and we provide different
training programs in order for them to develop new skills
